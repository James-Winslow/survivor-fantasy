Hardcore Mode

"Hardcore Mode" is a new mode available to the creators of private leagues. If your private league is in Hardcore Mode, we will restrict who can be traded in and out of your roster during redrafts.

During a redraft, if your survivors have been voted out they will not be tradeable for a survivor that is still in the game. The slot for that survivor will be locked on your roster. This setting applies to all players in that private league.

Blind Mode

"Blind Mode" is a new mode available to the creators of private leagues. If your private league is in Blind Mode, we will restrict the visible pick data to the last episode.

While waiting for the next episode to air, players will not be able to see the current picks that other players have in the league. Players will only be able to see the locked in picks for the last aired episode.
